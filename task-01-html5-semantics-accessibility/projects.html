Crop Disease Detection using CNN

Identifies plant diseases from leaf images using a convolutional neural network trained on PlantVillage dataset.

View details

Farmers often misidentify plant diseases, leading to wrong treatments and crop loss. Trained a MobileNetV2-based CNN on 54,000+ PlantVillage images to detect 15 disease types, achieving 93.7% validation accuracy. Exported as a TensorFlow Lite model with a mobile-friendly upload interface to keep it accessible on low-end devices.

Tech used: Python, TensorFlow, Keras, OpenCV, NumPy
